Project Goals

The AI-Content Filter aims to maintain authenticity on PeerDeck by identifying and downranking AI-generated content, ensuring student work reflects genuine effort and creativity. The project focuses on improving trust and safety in student communities.

Tech Stack

Built with Python and TensorFlow, leveraging LSTM models for text classification. Integrated with PeerDeck’s backend (Node.js, MongoDB) for real-time content filtering.

Impact

Enhances the credibility of student projects by prioritizing authentic contributions, supporting PeerDeck’s mission to connect students with real-world opportunities based on verified work.
