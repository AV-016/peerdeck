Description

The AI-Content Filter is an innovative solution to maintain content authenticity on platforms like PeerDeck. Using a TensorFlow-based NLP model, it detects AI-generated text by analyzing linguistic patterns, achieving high accuracy. Developed by Team Orion during an NLP course, this project enhances trust in user-generated content by identifying synthetic posts and enabling moderators to take action.
